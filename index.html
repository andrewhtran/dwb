The Roadmap

four phases, in order — no guesswork

Each phase gates the next. We don't scale spend until attribution is trustworthy, and we don't rebuild the site until the audits tell us exactly what's leaking.

Rx / Phase 01

diagnose

  • Full tech stack & website review
  • Full ad account audit across every active platform
  • Full data attribution and analytics audit
  • Baseline the numbers: what does a new customer actually cost today?
Rx / Phase 02

prescribe

  • Tech stack recommendations & consolidation
  • Revamp data attribution and reporting
  • Establish true cost per new customer as the north-star metric
  • Stand up the shared ad tracking sheet
Rx / Phase 03

treat

  • Concentrate spend on three platforms: Meta, Google, CTV
  • Scale UGC & whitelisted ads via commission-based creators
  • Launch the AI Skin Analysis lead magnet
  • Surge spend around Dr. Bowe's TV appearances
Rx / Phase 04

sustain

  • Revamp the full website — or at minimum, every product page
  • Fill the missing roles with the right contractors
  • Weekly automated reporting into Slack

Phase 03 · Treat

three platforms, full focus

Spreading budget across six channels means mastering none. We concentrate on the three where DWB's audience actually is — and where Dr. Bowe's media presence gives us an unfair advantage.

Platform / Meta

meta

The workhorse for skincare DTC. UGC, whitelisted creator ads, and clinical-proof creative, tested systematically through the tracking sheet.

Platform / Google

google + youtube

Capture high-intent search on Dr. Bowe's name, skin cycling, and category terms — plus YouTube, where her long-form authority content already lives.

Platform / CTV

ctv via vibe.co

Connected TV through Vibe.co, targeting the audiences of the exact shows Dr. Bowe appears on — GMA, Drew Barrymore, The View.

The Content Engine

a pipeline that doesn't bottleneck

The full content pipeline is mapped end-to-end here — and many of the roles in it can be combined into fewer, sharper hires.

right now: post-purchase video reviews

To ease pressure on the pipeline and cashflow immediately, we run a post-purchase offer incentivizing video reviews. Need volume fast? Blast the offer to the highest-engaged email segments — the creators are already customers.

at scale: commission-based ugc via trybe

Trybe scales UGC and whitelisted ads on a commission model — creators earn when they sell, so spend stays tied to performance.

  • Focus on 40+ influencers, matching DWB's core customer
  • Whitelisted ads run through creator handles for native trust

one studio, four disciplines

Find a creative or studio that handles photography, videography, graphic design, and video editing under one roof — with a good aesthetic eye, using AI for efficiency, never for deliverables.

design + development, one person

A UI/UX designer who is also a developer, specialized in Shopify and eCommerce, owns the product page and site revamp. I already have a vetted contractor for this.

The Lead Magnet

free ai skin analysis as the front door

A VISIA-style skin analysis, free, on the site — powered by Haut.ai. After demoing several competitors, Haut.ai wins clearly on simplicity and customization. It's the perfect lead magnet for a dermatologist-founded brand: diagnostic first, product second.

  1. Visitor uploads a selfie and gets an instant, personalized skin report
  2. DWB captures the email — plus rich data about that person's actual skin
  3. Retargeting ads and Klaviyo segments become dramatically more precise: barrier concerns see Bowe Glowe, texture concerns see Exfoliation Night
